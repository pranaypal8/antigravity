FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Categories

How extensive is the customization?

+
From collar spread to cuff barrel and initial monograms, every detail of our shirts can be tailored to your preference. Our interactive configurator allows for over 10,000 unique combinations.

What if the fit isn't perfect?

+
We offer a Perfect Fit Guarantee. If your first shirt doesn't fit exactly as desired, we will remake it from scratch free of charge, or cover local alterations up to ₹3,500.

What is the delivery timeline?

+
Each shirt is made fresh after your order is placed. Standard delivery takes 10–14 business days. Express production (7 days) is available for an additional charge. We ship pan-India via Shiprocket with real-time tracking.

Are custom items returnable?

+
Because every garment is made specifically for you, we do not accept returns due to change of mind. However, if there is a quality defect or a sizing error on our part, we will remake the garment at no charge.

Do you offer Bespoke Gift Sets?

+
Yes. Our signature gift experience allows the recipient to design their own masterpiece. Gift cards come with premium packaging and a handwritten note from our atelier.

Which payment methods are accepted?

+
We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay — UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, and EMI options. All transactions are 256-bit SSL encrypted.
